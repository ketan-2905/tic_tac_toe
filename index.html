  • Goal: Form a straight line of three of your marks—either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—to win the game.
  • Taking Turns: Players alternate turns, placing their mark (X or O) in an empty box on the 3x3 grid.
  • Three-Mark Rule: Each player can only have three marks on the board. Once you place your fourth mark, the oldest one disappears, keeping you with three, making room for new strategies as you play.
  • Victory: Keep aligning your marks while managing the disappearing marks—first player to form a line of three wins the game!
